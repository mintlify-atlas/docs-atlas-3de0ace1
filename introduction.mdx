This archive preserves materials released by the U.S. National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) in connection with its investigation of China Eastern Airlines Flight MU5735 (NTSB case **DCA22WA102**). The materials include official FOIA response reports, cockpit voice recorder (CVR) and flight data recorder (FDR) recovery data, and an unofficial Chinese translation of the combined recorder download report.
