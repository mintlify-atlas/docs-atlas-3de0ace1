This is the NTSB combined CVR/FDR download report for case DCA22WA102, dated July 1, 2022. Prepared by the Office of Research and Engineering in Washington, DC, the report documents the recovery process for both recorders from a severely damaged Boeing 737-800 (registration B-1791) that crashed near Wuzhou, China on March 21, 2022. The report covers physical damage assessment, multi-stage download attempts, chip-level recovery procedures, and the final audio and flight data outputs provided to the Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC).

## Report personnel

The data recovery group was formed on March 28, 2022, composed of NTSB and CAAC representatives. All handling of the storage modules was performed by NTSB personnel and witnessed by CAAC.

## Recorder equipment

Both recorders are Honeywell HFR5-series solid-state units designed to meet EUROCAE ED-112A crash-survivability requirements.
