This section provides an unofficial Chinese translation of the NTSB DCA22WA102 Cockpit Voice and Flight Data Recorder Combined Download Report, dated July 1, 2022. The translation was produced by GitHub user `wrongly-cuddly-obsession` and is released under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license. It is intended to make the technical findings more accessible to Chinese-speaking readers while preserving the structure and meaning of the original NTSB document.
