This section gives you access to the complete set of Freedom of Information Act materials released by the U.S. National Transportation Safety Board for the MU5735 investigation (NTSB case DCA22WA102). The documents here span official FOIA response reports, correspondence between the requester and the NTSB, and supplementary flight recorder data files.

## What this archive contains

The materials are organized into the following categories:

- **PDF reports** — Seven NTSB FOIA response documents (`report.pdf` through `report (6).pdf`) plus `rzcommu.pdf`, a supplementary communication document covering recorder analysis and investigation findings.
- **Response letters** — Scanned PNG images of the official NTSB FOIA correspondence (`res_letter_1.png`, `res_letter_2.png`, `res_letter_3.png`).
- **CSV data files** — `ExactSample.csv` contains FDR measurements at exact sample timestamps; `TableResolution.csv` provides the same data aligned to 1/16-second resolution.
- **UPK data package** — `12minute.upk` is a binary FDR data package covering the corrected final 12 minutes of flight.
