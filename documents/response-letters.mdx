## What FOIA response letters contain

NTSB FOIA response letters typically include:

- **Acknowledgement of the request** — confirmation that the NTSB received the FOIA submission and assigned it a tracking number.
- **Case number** — the NTSB accident docket identifier (in this case, DCA22WA102) used to locate materials in the NTSB public docket system.
- **Description of released materials** — a list or description of the documents and data files being provided in response to the request.
- **Withholding notices** — any determination that certain records are exempt from disclosure under FOIA exemptions (for example, privacy or deliberative process protections), along with the legal basis.
- **Appeal rights** — information on how to challenge a partial or full denial of the request.

<Info>
  The NTSB processes FOIA requests under 49 C.F.R. Part 801 and the Freedom of Information Act, 5 U.S.C. § 552. Requests for accident investigation materials are handled separately from the agency's public docket releases. Response times and the scope of released materials depend on the nature of the request and the status of the investigation at the time of filing.
</Info>
