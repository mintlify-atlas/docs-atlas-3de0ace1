These are the official documents released by the U.S. National Transportation Safety Board in response to Freedom of Information Act requests for materials related to China Eastern Airlines Flight MU5735 (NTSB case DCA22WA102). The reports cover investigation findings, recorder analysis, and related correspondence from the NTSB's involvement as a foreign accredited representative on the Chinese investigation.

## Binary data package

The repository also includes `12minute.upk`, a binary Flight Data Recorder data package containing corrected data for the final 12 minutes of flight. This file uses the UPK format and is intended for use with specialized FDR analysis software. It is not a PDF and cannot be opened in a standard document viewer.
