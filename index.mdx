This archive preserves Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) materials released by the U.S. National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) in connection with its investigation of China Eastern Airlines Flight MU5735 — a Boeing 737-800 (registration B-1791) that crashed near Wuzhou, China on March 21, 2022. The repository includes official FOIA response documents, raw flight recorder data, and an unofficial Chinese translation of the NTSB recorder report.

## What is in this archive

The repository contains materials released through FOIA requests directed at the NTSB. The original files were shared publicly by another GitHub user whose repository was subsequently deleted or made private; they have been re-archived here to preserve public access.
